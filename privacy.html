1. Overview & Scope

Qentro ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website (qentro.com), mobile applications, in-store events, and any related services (collectively, the "Services"). It explains your choices and rights regarding your personal data. By interacting with Qentro, you acknowledge the practices described herein.

🔒 Our commitment: We never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is used to enhance your shopping experience and improve our products.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with seamless service, we collect several types of information:

We may also collect aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you for statistical analysis.

3. How We Use Your Information

Qentro uses your personal data only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Order processing & delivery: Manage purchases, shipments, returns, and customer service.
  • Account management: Maintain your profile, order history, and preferences.
  • Marketing & personalization: With your opt-in consent, send promotional offers, style updates, and new arrivals. You may unsubscribe anytime.
  • Service improvement: Analyze trends, track website performance, and enhance user experience.
  • Legal compliance: Prevent fraud, comply with applicable laws, and enforce our Terms.

4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and local storage to remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookie settings through your browser; however, disabling some cookies may impact functionality (e.g., cart retention). Qentro also uses essential cookies for secure checkout.

  • Essential cookies: Required for navigation, login, and purchases.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand user behavior (e.g., Google Analytics anonymized).
  • Preference cookies: Remember language and region.

For more details, see our Cookie Preferences tool (available in footer).

5. Sharing & Disclosure of Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share information with trusted third parties under these circumstances:

  • Service providers: Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), shipping carriers (FedEx, DHL), and IT infrastructure (cloud hosting). They are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Business transfers: If Qentro undergoes merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred with notice.
  • Legal requirements: To comply with subpoenas, court orders, or governmental requests, or to protect the rights and safety of Qentro and its users.
📦 Third-party links: Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices; please review their policies.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR for EEA, CCPA for California), you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure: Ask us to delete your personal data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Restriction / Objection: Limit processing or object to certain uses (e.g., direct marketing).
  • Data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@qentro.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Data Security

Qentro implements robust technical and organizational measures: SSL encryption, restricted access, regular vulnerability scans, and secure data centers. While no system is 100% secure, we strive to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and authorities as required by law.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy — typically, for the duration of your account activity and up to 7 years after your last interaction for tax, legal, and fraud prevention reasons. Anonymized analytics data may be kept longer. When no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize your information.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 16 or older. Qentro does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately so we can delete such data.

10. International Data Transfers

Qentro operates globally. Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. When we transfer data, we rely on standard contractual clauses, adequacy decisions, or other lawful mechanisms to ensure adequate protection. By using Qentro, you consent to such transfers.
