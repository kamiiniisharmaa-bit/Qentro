Our mission: Less, but better

We are committed to reducing waste through durable materials, ethical production, and transparent partnerships. Each Qentro piece is crafted to last for years, not seasons. We believe in a future where style and sustainability coexist seamlessly.

What guides us

Ethical craft

We partner with B-Certified workshops and ensure fair wages. Every stitch is made with respect for people and planet.

Timeless design

Silhouettes and forms that transcend trends. Qentro objects blend with any space, any era.

Circular future

Repair, reuse, recycle. Our take-back program gives old Qentro items a second life.

Community first

We listen, co-create, and celebrate the people who inspire our collections — including you.

From a small studio to a global collective

What started in a sunlit garage in Ojai, California, quickly became a movement. Our founder, Elena Marchetti, envisioned a brand where utility, poetry, and environmental consciousness intersect. Within three years, Qentro has grown into a tight-knit team of designers, artisans, and storytellers who share a radical commitment to honest design.

We don't follow fashion calendars. Instead, we release curated collections when they're ready — each piece tested for real life. Today, Qentro ships to over 40 countries, yet we retain the soul of a small atelier.

Material integrity

We source GOTS-certified cottons, recycled nylon, plant-based leathers, and Forest Stewardship timber for our lifestyle goods. Each material is chosen for durability and low environmental footprint. Our goal: 95% circular materials by 2030.
